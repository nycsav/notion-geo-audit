the problem

AI answers are the new front page.

When someone asks ChatGPT "who should I hire near me?" — either you're in the answer, or your competitor is.

Google had SEO. AI search has GEO — Generative Engine Optimization. Almost nobody is checking theirs.

what the app does

15 checks. 4 questions.

1 · Reach

Can AI find you?

Are AI crawlers welcomed or blocked? Is there a sitemap, an llms.txt?

2 · Identity

Does AI know who you are?

Structured data that says "this is us" — so you're never confused with someone else.

3 · Voice

Can AI quote you?

One clear headline. One sentence that defines you. FAQs machines can lift.

4 · Access

Can AI connect?

Feeds and agent endpoints — the plumbing of the agentic web.

notion platform · workers

Run it inside Notion.

Notion Workers are a hosted runtime — your code runs on Notion's servers, no laptop needed. Four steps, on screen:

# 1 · install the Notion CLI (once)
curl -fsSL https://ntn.dev | bash
# 2 · sign in to your workspace
ntn login
# 3 · deploy the geo-audit worker
cd worker && ntn workers deploy
# 4 · in Notion: Settings → Connections → add auditSite to your Custom Agent

Workers are free in beta — they run on Notion credits starting Aug 11.

notion platform · agents · sync · template

Three Notion-native superpowers.

1 · Custom Agent tool

Ask your agent, in Notion

The worker's auditSite tool appears in your Custom Agent. In Notion chat: "Audit brainstation.io and add it to the scoreboard." Done.

2 · Scheduled sync

The scoreboard refreshes itself

scoreboardSync re-audits every row on Notion's schedule (default every 30 min). Fix your site, watch your score climb — automatically.
